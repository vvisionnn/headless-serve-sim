Depth · Motion · Breakpoints

Depth: no structural shadows. Layer by fill (#000 → #1d1d1f → #161617) and 1px hairlines. Real shadow (rgba(0,0,0,.5)) only for overlays.

Motion: ~300ms, cubic-bezier(0.4,0,0.6,1) decelerate. Nav color fade 0.32s. Never bouncy.

Breakpoints: 320 · 734/735 · 1068/1069 · 1440. Content max 980px, fluid 87.5%.
